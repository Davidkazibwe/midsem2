About BASS

BASS was founded in 1999 with a mission to provide holistic Christian Education in order to enable students to make a positive contribution to society for eternity. We believe in nurturing a community of respect, innovation, and excellence. Our dedicated faculty is committed to providing personalized attention and guidance to help each student reach their full potential.

Our Values

  • Academic Excellence
  • Character Development
  • Diversity and Inclusion
  • Community Engagement
  • Lifelong Learning

Academics

BASS offers a comprehensive academic program that prepares students for college success. We offer a variety of courses in core subjects like:

  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Social Studies
  • Foreign Languages

We also offer a range of elective courses in areas such as:

  • Arts
  • Technology
  • Business
  • Physical Education
  • And more!
